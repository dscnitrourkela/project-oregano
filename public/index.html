HackNitr is one of a kind hackathon which endeavors to bring and integrate talent, innovation, and creativity under one roof to build solutions for real-world problems using the latest technologies. It aims to provide student developers from across the country, a platform to transform their ideas into prototypes and solve the enigmas.

Hack for Social Good

With the rapid advancement of technology, it is important to use its power in ways that benefit society. HackDavis challenges its participants to hack for social good, and create an opportunity for us to explore the intersection between technology and society.

Why Hack with us?

Whether you’re writing your first lines of HTML, building a VR app or a hoverboard, HackNITR is just the place for you. We will have workshops and mentors to help make your idea happen. You will be able to exhibit your skills without worrying about any mortal needs. We will take care of everything

Health

Develop a product that encourages people to live a healthier lifestyle. Help predict and prevent illnesses before they affect the body.

Environment

Aggregate public data to analyze or improve government green energy policies. Design solutions to reduce energy consumption in habitable spaces.

Education

Discover new ways to faciliatate learning within the classroom. Enable accessible education for individuals with learning disabilities.
